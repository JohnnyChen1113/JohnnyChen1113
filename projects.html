Projects

Evolution of Transcription Initiation in Fungi

Doctoral Research · PI: Dr. Zhenguo Lin · NSF Funded

Investigating the mechanisms and evolution of transcription start site (TSS) selection across fungal species. Performed large-scale comparative genomic analysis of TSS evolution across 70+ fungal species using CAGE-seq and comparative genomics approaches. Led the construction of TSSHub, a genomic database for transcription initiation studies across eukaryotes.

Outcomes: Published S. bayanus genome on Genome Research as co-first author; 3 first-author manuscripts in preparation.

CAGE-seq Comparative Genomics Fungi

Plant DNA N6-Methyladenine (6mA) Demethylase

Collaborative Project · with Dr. Wenyan Xiao · NIH R15 Funded

Developed bioinformatics pipelines for PacBio SMRT and Oxford Nanopore long-read sequencing datasets to enable genome-wide DNA 6mA profiling in model plant Arabidopsis thaliana.

Outcomes: Co-first author manuscript in preparation.

Oxford Nanopore PacBio SMRT Epigenetics

Western Painted Turtle Genome and Isoform Resource

Collaborative Project · with Dr. Daniel Warren · NSF Funded

Assembled a high-quality, chromosome-level reference genome for the western painted turtle (Chrysemys picta bellii) and performed PacBio Iso-Seq–based transcriptome analysis across multiple tissues and developmental stages. Generated a comprehensive isoform-level annotation resource for studying the genetic basis of hypoxia/anoxia tolerance and for broader functional and comparative genomics.

Outcomes: First author manuscript under review.
