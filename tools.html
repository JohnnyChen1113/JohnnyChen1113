Developed Tools

TSSr

An R/Bioconductor package designed for the comprehensive analysis of transcription start site (TSS) data. It provides a suite of tools for identification, visualization, and annotation of TSSs.

R Bioconductor TSS

autoBSgenome

A Python wrapper for BSgenome that simplifies the creation of BSgenome packages. It automates the process, allowing users to build custom genome packages as easily as filling out a questionnaire.

Python BSgenome Automation

DataColor

A tool for unveiling biological data relationships through distinctive color mapping. It helps researchers visualize complex datasets with intuitive and informative color schemes.

Visualization Color Mapping Genomics

PhyloForge

A comprehensive toolkit for unifying micro- and macro-evolutionary studies using genomic signals. It facilitates the construction of phylogenetic trees from various genomic data sources.

Phylogenetics Evolution Genomics

Bioinformatics Databases

Water Lily Pond

A multiomics database for water lilies, providing comprehensive genomic and transcriptomic resources to support research on early flowering plant evolution.

2025 Multiomics Water Lily

GROP

Genomic Repository for Oil Plants (GROP) is a comprehensive database designed to facilitate the study of oil-producing plants through genomic data integration.

2022 Oil Plants Genomics

Portal of Juglandaceae

A comprehensive platform for Juglandaceae study, integrating genomic, transcriptomic, and phenotypic data for walnut and related species.

2020 Juglandaceae Walnut

MGH (Maca Genome Hub)

A genome hub for the medicinal plant Maca (Lepidium meyenii), providing genomic resources to understand its adaptation and medicinal properties.

2018 Maca Medicinal Plant

realDB

A genome and transcriptome resource for red algae (phylum Rhodophyta), facilitating comparative genomics and evolutionary studies of red algae.

2018 Red Algae Transcriptomics
